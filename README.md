# Acta

## Introduction

Acta is a powerful tool for managing and organizing your tasks and projects. With its intuitive interface and advanced features, Acta helps you stay on top of your work and achieve your goals.

## Features

Acta offers a wide range of features to help you manage your tasks and projects efficiently. Some of the key features include:

- Task management: Create, edit, and delete tasks with ease.
- Project management: Organize your tasks into projects and track their progress.
- Time tracking: Keep track of how much time you spend on each task.
- Collaboration: Share your tasks and projects with others and collaborate on them in real-time.
- Customization: Customize your Acta experience with themes, layouts, and more.
